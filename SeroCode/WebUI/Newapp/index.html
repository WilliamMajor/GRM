About Project & Robot

GPS-Guided RFID Roof Moisture Mapping Robot.

The project handles issues related to moisture buildup on industrial and commercial building rooftops.

Trapped moisture often damages these types of buildings and is extremely expensive for business owners to deal with. This is because industrial and commercial rooftops tend to be flat, unlike residential roofs, which are designed to have rain slip off onto the ground. The buildings our project is designed for are too wide, so creating an angled roof would be impractical and expensive due to the extra building material required.

Oftentimes, moisture isnt detectable from the surface of the tile. Traditional methods of measuring this moisture require drilling into the tile, which obviously causes damage and potential for future problems. Additionally, multiple samples are required in order to accurately measure the moisture damage of a roof.

Some construction companies are implementing RFID-embedded tiles for rooftop construction. When these RFID tags are primed with an RF signal at a specific power level and frequency, the moisture level of the tile is returned. Our goal is to create an autonomous robot to navigate the rooftops of commercial and industrial buildings to map its moisture using these RFID tags. An extremely accurate moisture map can be produced and transmitted off-site to maintenance crews to repair damage

before it becomes significant. Also, our method doesn't require any damage to the rooftop, and hardly any interaction from people.
